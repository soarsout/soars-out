Soars Out

Soars Out is a youth-led social enterprise and charitable initiative based in South Auckland, dedicated to empowering rangatahi to succeed beyond the classroom. We bridge the gap between secondary school and the real world, equipping young people with the skills, confidence, and leadership needed to navigate future opportunities and challenges.

Our Vision

Our Vision is for rangatahi in Aotearoa New Zealand to soar beyond school, supported by collective strength, practical skills, and pathways that enable them to define and achieve success on their own terms.
