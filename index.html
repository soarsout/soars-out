"Failing to plan is planning to fail"

Ineffective study habits are the things that stop people from learning and succeeding. Ineffective study habits occur for many reasons such as distractions due to social media, lack of motivation, no routine or study plan, multitasking and procrastination etc. Another main reason ineffective study habits may occur is due to the lack of knowledge of not understanding how to study or what to study, it may also be because of the amount of work being set to students and adults which causes them to feel overwhelmed and stressed. Addressing these ineffective study habits is important in order for students and adults to learn without any barriers stopping them from moving forward. Additionally, students and adults to learn effectively we can start by adding small changes to our daily study life such as study goals and a study timetable. Overall, looking out for these ineffective study habits and being able to find solutions will be life-changing for students and adults.
