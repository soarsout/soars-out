"Sky's The Limit!"

Soars Out is a youth-led social enterprise and charitable initiative based in South Auckland, dedicated to empowering rangatahi to succeed beyond the classroom. We bridge the gap between secondary school and the real world, equipping young people with the skills, confidence, and leadership needed to navigate future opportunities and challenges.

Mission and Purpose:

Soars Out exists to uplift, inspire, and activate the next generation of South Auckland leaders. We provide a safe, culturally grounded space where rangatahi can explore their potential and build real-world skills. Our kaupapa is about ensuring young people feel seen, heard, and supported as they navigate education, work, money, wellbeing, and leadership.

What We Do:

Practical Skill-Building: Workshops, online sessions, and community events that cover financial literacy, leadership, digital skills, wellbeing, and career pathways. Youth Leadership: Our programmes are youth-led and youth-designed, we centre rangatahi voices in everything we do. Mentoring and Support: One-on-one and group support to help young people plan their futures, build confidence, and step into opportunities. Community Advocacy: We amplify youth voices in local decision-making, especially through spaces like the Otahuhu Youth Panel and community partnerships.

Our Approach:

We prioritise lived experience, recognising that rangatahi know what rangatahi need. Rooted in South Auckland values - Whanaungatanga, Manaakitanga, and Tino Rangatiratanga. Blending online and in-person learning, ensuring accessibility for busy students, workers, and young parents. Providing content that is honest, culturally aware, and actually relevant to the realities of young people today.

Why Soars Out Matters:

Many young people feel unprepared for adulthood - we change that. We fill the gap between school and the real world with skills you don't get taught in class: financial literacy, workforce, and university management. We open pathways to opportunities through connections, mentoring, and community networks. We help build confident, capable, and future-ready rangatahi who can take up space and truly take their place.

Our Vision:

A future where every young person has the tools, support, and belief in themselves to thrive, lead, and SOAR OUT into their full potential.
